## Introduction
SuayLang addresses the lack of scorable, reviewer-auditable contracts for backend equivalence and diagnostics stability in small languages. We operationalize these claims as executable artifacts.

## Method
Deterministic, multi-seed differential testing, golden diagnostics, coverage reporting, and mutation/injection validation. All results are saved as artifacts and regenerated by `make research`.

## Experimental Setup
Baseline: Python as reference, same programs, parse/compile/interp/vm times. Ablation: toggles for diagnostics contract, generator mode, VM/interp. Mutation: injected faults, stress tests.
